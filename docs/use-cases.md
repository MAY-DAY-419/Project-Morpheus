Purpose: Short, judge-friendly bullets describing who benefits and how.

Primary use cases

- Anonymous Individual Report
  - Description: An employee submits a harassment complaint without creating an account.
  - Why it matters: Removes fear of retaliation and reduces underreporting.

- Bypass HR (HR-is-the-Harasser)
  - Description: Reporter selects external NGOs or ICCs; HR access omitted.
  - Why it matters: Prevents suppression when HR is conflicted.

- Multi-Authority Access
  - Description: Reporter chooses a combination of authorities (HR + NGO or ICC only).
  - Why it matters: Shared responsibility prevents unilateral deletion.

- Pattern Detection (Safe Analytics)
  - Description: Platform aggregates anonymized metadata (time windows, department tags) to surface repeat offenders or hotspots.
  - Why it matters: Supports systemic remediation while preserving reporter anonymity.

Secondary (future)

- Legal Escalation Workflow
  - Description: With consent, authorities can request legal take-up; system preserves chain-of-custody via encrypted audit logs.

- Anonymous Follow-up Messaging
  - Description: Authorities can send messages to the reporter via the platform without learning identity (one-way or token-based reply).
